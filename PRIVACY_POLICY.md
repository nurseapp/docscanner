# Privacy Policy for DocScanner

**Last Updated**: January 10, 2026

## Introduction

DocScanner ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Scanned Documents**: Images and text extracted from documents you scan
- **Document Metadata**: Names, dates, and categories you assign to documents
- **User Preferences**: Settings and configurations within the app

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App features used, crash reports, performance data
- **Camera Access**: Required for document scanning (processed locally)
- **Storage Access**: Required to save and retrieve documents (stored locally)

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Processing
- All document scanning and OCR processing happens **on your device**
- Documents are stored locally on your device
- We do not upload or store your documents on our servers

### Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:

**OpenAI API**
- Purpose: AI-powered OCR and document analysis
- Data Sent: Images of documents you choose to scan
- Data Retention: OpenAI may temporarily process images for analysis but does not store them
- Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- All scanned documents are stored locally on your device
- Data is stored using your device's secure storage mechanisms
- You can delete documents at any time through the app

### Cloud Storage
- We do **not** automatically upload your documents to any cloud service
- Any cloud backup is managed by your device's operating system (e.g., Android backup)

## Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:
- **PIN Protection**: Optional PIN lock for sensitive documents
- **Local Encryption**: Sensitive data is encrypted on your device
- **Secure Communication**: All API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not sell or share your data with third parties

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored by the app
- **Delete**: Remove any or all documents from the app
- **Export**: Export your documents in various formats
- **Control**: Manage permissions and settings

## Permissions Explained

### Camera
- **Purpose**: To scan documents and capture QR codes
- **Access**: Only when you actively use scanning features
- **Data Usage**: Images are processed locally or sent to OpenAI for OCR

### Photo Library
- **Purpose**: To import existing images as documents
- **Access**: Only when you choose to import images
- **Data Usage**: Selected images are processed locally

### Storage
- **Purpose**: To save scanned documents on your device
- **Access**: Required for app functionality
- **Data Usage**: Documents are saved in app-specific storage

## Children's Privacy

DocScanner is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Showing an in-app notification
- Posting the new policy in the app store listing

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data:
1. Open DocScanner
2. Go to Settings
3. Select "Clear All Data"
4. Confirm deletion

Alternatively, uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

## International Users

DocScanner is available worldwide. Your data is processed locally on your device, regardless of your location. API calls to OpenAI may route through their international infrastructure.
